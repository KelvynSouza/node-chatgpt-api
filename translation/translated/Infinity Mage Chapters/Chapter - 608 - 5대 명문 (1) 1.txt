Winter has come.

Alpheus, who was looking down at the snow-covered school landscape, turned from the window and asked.

"Did you get a document from the association?"

"See for yourself."

The stiff document that Olivia had placed on the desk caught his eye.

It was about how the graduating students of the five prestigious magic schools had to personally deliver their graduation exam participation forms to the association.

"This could be a variable too."

It was the time when the final 30-week evaluation began.

At this time, the students' minds become sharp as a knife, and they adjust their biorhythms to peak on the day of the graduation exam.

In the meantime, going to the Magic Association in the capital would have been quite stressful.

"Are they using students for politics?"

It was something that happened when Luphirst, who had been acting in Gauld's place, officially took the position of the head of the Magic Association.

"I guess so. Luphirst is a person with an elite-first philosophy. It's probably part of a plan to manage the students who will join the association in the future."

That's why they narrowed the scope to just the five prestigious schools.

"But isn't that too much? Changing it like this, when until last year the school used to substitute for it. The backlash must have been not small, right?"

"It's a kind of power show. Luphirst's connections are excellent, but he hasn't completely taken over the association."

"Gauld's influence must have been bigger than we thought."

"He was a madman, but he was the strongest wizard in the kingdom. Even though they criticized his actions on the surface, there must have been quite a few wizards who supported him inside."

Gauld was such a man.

"Yeah. Let's leave politics to politicians, and we teachers should do things for the students."

Colly, who had been listening to the conversation, opened his mouth.

"First of all, if we're going to send 30 people to the association, we need to fill our quota too. Currently, Anchar has dropped out and one seat is vacant, and Aider also needs to make a decision."

Aider, who had suffered a full-body fracture in the Ataraxia of Shironne during the high ground occupation evaluation, was still hospitalized in the school infirmary.

The treatment was over, but the problem was mental.

"According to the medical teacher, the trauma has almost been treated. Aider is also showing enthusiasm to participate in the graduation exam."

"Hmm."

The graduating class was given the right to participate in the graduation exam, and if they wanted to use their rights, the school couldn't stop them.

"But isn't he too young? The fact that the treatment took more than half a year means that the trauma was quite severe."

Alpheus agreed with Olivia's words.

"Let's check one last time. If there's no problem, let's raise one applicant from the advanced class to fill the quota. It should be fine to give the qualification in order of grades."

"Yes. I'll leave it to Teacher Shiina."

Colly bowed his head and left the principal's office.

As always, Shironne was lying on the floor of the research meeting.

Since the upper part of Istas no longer existed, he came here every day and expanded the sequence formula.

"Believe in the power of the day."

Nade, who had ended his heartache, was also receiving a normal evaluation, and the students who were paying the price of the Scramble Royal were also dropping their grades smoothly.

Now there were only 10 days left until the graduation exam.

All the training done so far was nothing more than a process to see the results of that day.

"Kueuuuu!"

As the sequence formula exceeded 200, Shironne's face was horribly crumpled.

He had manifested a sublimation explosion at the level of the path, and now he had come 200 times further than that.

But still, jumping over the path and entering the realm of the sun was still difficult.

'It's tremendous. There's no end.'

Even if he reached out to catch it, all he could see was the tunnel of numbers running forever.

-There is an end to the numbers.

He recalled Gepin's words, which he still couldn't understand, and accelerated the sequence formula. The time energy that filled the research meeting whirled around and distorted the scenery so that even the shape could not be distinguished.

'Go!'

He will reach out endlessly.

"Yeah. But the travel time won't be that long. We're going to take a space teleportation magic circle from the Creas branch of the Magic Association to the capital."

"Hey, are you still holding onto that? I'm really sorry. I was out of line."

"The rehabilitation was successful. He doesn't have nightmares and the hallucinations of seeing light are gone. But......"

"It's too clean. I couldn't catch anything strange in the interview, but maybe it's a doctor's intuition?"

"I called Shirone to check that."

"We're going to test now. If Shirone reproduces the situation at that time, the medical teacher will check Aider's condition."

"At that time?"

"Don't worry. It's not a real attack. Can you do it?"

"Yes, of course. I'm all better now."
