"How about it, Shirone? Can you avoid this too?"

"Kruk!"

A powerful electric beam shot out, and with a sharp noise of electric charges colliding, the air where the electricity passed burned completely.

The attack followed Shirone wherever she dodged. The photon output from the photon mansion was tracking Shirone's coordinates in real time.

"Ah..."

Shirone felt dizzy. She didn't feel like she was fighting a person. It felt like fighting an armed warship with countless weapons.

Shirone dodged the ice spear that stabbed vertically by rolling on the ground. As the world spun around, she heard a burning sound near the ground. She activated photonization magic and bounced her waist, and almost at the same time, an atomic bomb exploded.

The students were stunned by Dante's tremendous firepower.

"Wow... What kind of magic is that?"

Mark also thought it was absurd. Even if Pascal did parallel processing, the brain of a human controlled the whole thing. It was beyond human ability to manage hundreds of magic circles.

Iruki looked back at Nade. He seemed to have realized it already by his unprecedented serious expression.

"Nade, say something. I don't get it."

"That's... an automaton."

"Automaton?"

"An automated mechanical device. You mesh several gears together and then move the first gear. Then the last gear gives a fixed value. It's a simple mechanism, but if this process increases to astronomical units, it can do things that look very difficult. Like tracking Shirone's coordinates with photon output, or switching mansions at high speed."

Shirone experienced the power of the automaton firsthand. No matter how she responded, the automaton automatically calculated and responded. The automaton was an impregnable fortress.

It was basic magic, but the power enhanced by mansions was not basic magic at all. Shirone dodged the bombardment and rolled on the ground, then hurriedly looked for Dante, but he was buried under hundreds of magic circles and invisible.

The magic circles floating around felt like the eyes of a monster with hundreds of eyes.

"Uuuuuu!"

Shirone felt creepy and fired photon cannons like crazy. The automaton reacted immediately. A lightning cannon charged from the bolt mansion shot out, and a blinding current carved a flash of lightning in space, bursting the photon cannon like a balloon.

The lightning cannon grazed Shirone's side, and that alone triggered anti-magic. As Shirone staggered, the automaton poured out all the magic it could use. Countless magics that could not be counted by hand incinerated everything within radius.

Pascal, who had exhausted all his available energy, made a strange noise and stopped moving. As the smoke cleared, Shirone's figure holding one shoulder appeared. A moment later, her knees wobbled and bent, and finally slammed into the ground.

"This time, Shirone went down."

One down was not the problem. The fact that anti-magic hit her squarely meant that Shirone's durability had cracked, and that meant the end of immortal function.

"That's really amazing."
